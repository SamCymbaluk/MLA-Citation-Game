MLA 8th Edition Citation Practice

This drag and drop activity will allow you to practice building citations using the template provided by the Modern Language Association. The 8th edition of the MLA now suggests that a standardized template be used to build all citations.

Instructions- You will be provided with a number of attempts to practice how to correctly organize the components of a citation. You will drag and drop each part of the citation into the correct order. Remember to use the link to the MLA 8th edition template on the header ribbon of your screen to help you. Hints will be provided to you. Be aware checking hints will deduct marks from your final score on this activity.

You will also be given instructions to help you create an in-text citation you would use when quoting or paraphrasing from the same source. You must type in an answer in the in-text citation box provided. This in-text citation will also be graded.

Once you have completed either 10 or 20 citations/in-text citations you will click submit quiz to see your final mark. Please ask the Teacher-Librarian for your 6 character classroom ID to use so that your mark can be properly submitted to your teachers.
